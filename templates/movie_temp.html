ABOUT

Movie Selection Made Easy

The purpose of this app is to recommend movies to the user based on their choices to a short quiz. Often times when people use medias like Netflix, they are suggested movies based on a specific genre or other characteristics. However, we believe that our users should be recommended movies based on multiple characteristics of movies for more quality movie recommendations. Users also don't want to have to scroll 100s of different options. That's why we are recommending a few options which we believe will really appeal to the user's preferences.
